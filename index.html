About MSP

Minimum Support Price (MSP) is a vital market intervention by the Government of India to safeguard agricultural producers against sharp falls in farm prices. This guarantee ensures fair compensation for farmers and promotes sustainable agriculture practices.

Our initiative focuses on providing transparent pricing information and support for local farmers, helping them make informed decisions about their crops.

Importance of Horticulture

Horticulture plays a crucial role in South Indian agriculture, contributing significantly to food security, nutrition, and economic opportunities. Our focus is on preserving and promoting local varieties of fruits and vegetables.

Through MSP support, we help maintain biodiversity in agricultural practices while ensuring farmers receive fair compensation for their produce.
