About Jallikkattu MSP

Welcome to Jallikkattu MSP - Your trusted platform for agricultural price support and market connectivity. We are dedicated to empowering farmers across Tamil Nadu by providing transparent Minimum Support Price (MSP) information and direct market access.

MSP Support

We ensure farmers receive fair prices for their produce through government-backed Minimum Support Price mechanisms.

Market Access

Direct connection between farmers and buyers, eliminating middlemen and ensuring better returns for agricultural produce.

Price Transparency

Real-time updates on MSP rates and market prices for various agricultural products across Tamil Nadu.

Our Mission

To revolutionize agricultural marketing by providing a transparent, efficient, and farmer-friendly platform that ensures fair prices and sustainable growth for the farming community.
